The Art of Political Parody & Satire

Visual communication is CJP’s primary tool for bypassing political rhetoric and connecting directly with India's youth. Our design system merges bold editorial vector typography, high-impact contrast colors, retro protest poster aesthetics, and satirical heraldry.

By reclaiming the derogatory label 'cockroaches' as an emblem of survival and resilience, CJP visuals resonate across Instagram, X (Twitter), Reddit, and university campus notice boards nationwide.

Open-Source Student Toolkit

All CJP design files, vector emblems, posters, and stickers are released under open Creative Commons licenses. Students across states can freely download high-res files, print them locally, and display them during campus assemblies and local rallies.

Our open visual repository ensures that every student, regardless of background or region, can participate in creating protest banners and spreading the 5-point manifesto.

Street Art & Jantar Mantar Exhibitions

During ground demonstrations at Jantar Mantar, New Delhi, CJP sets up live street art exhibitions where student artists display screen-printed banners, satirical cartoons, and physical art installations questioning paper leak scandals and judicial independence.

Explore our complete digital gallery, download high-definition vector artwork, and share these visuals across your social handles to support youth civic rights.
