The Art of Political Parody

Visual communication is CJP’s primary tool for bypassing complex political jargon and connecting directly with youth. Our design language merges bold editorial styles, retro protest poster aesthetics, and high-impact digital art. By utilizing high-contrast maroon, bold lettering, and the symbolic cockroach emblem, we create graphics that stand out in crowded social media feeds.

All CJP design files, vectors, and stickers are released under open-source creative commons licenses, allowing students to download, modify, print, and distribute them freely on their campuses.

On-Ground Street Exhibition

During physical rallies at Jantar Mantar, we set up a live street art gallery. Students display handmade satirical protest posters, screen-printed banners, and stickers. We encourage young artists to contribute to the movement's visual inventory by submitting their satire art directly to our campus cells.

Our imagery aims to evoke resilient humor in the face of institutional arrogance. Explore, download, print, and share our digital toolkit to keep the protest alive.
