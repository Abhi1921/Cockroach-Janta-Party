Vol 2 · Public filing

The manifesto without the fluff.

Sponsored by no one. Written for people who still share PDFs of RTI replies in group chats at 2 a.m.

  1. 01
    Publish education spending

    Budgets, utilization, and vacancy data online — updated quarterly.

  2. 02
    End exploitative unpaid work

    Internships that teach must also pay. Hiring rules must be public.

  3. 03
    Name every public contract

    Who got what, for how much, by when — searchable.

  4. 04
    Defend digital citizenship

    Consent, access, and redress when systems fail people.

  5. 05
    Institutionalize youth voice

    Seats on boards and consultations that actually change outcomes.
