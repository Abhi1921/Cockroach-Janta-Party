Origin story

From an insult to a movement.

On 15 May 2026, Chief Justice of India Surya Kant’s courtroom remarks comparing some unemployed youth and activists to “cockroaches” and “parasites” triggered national outrage. The next day — 16 May 2026 — Abhijeet Dipke launched the Cockroach Janta Party as satire that stuck.

Founder

Abhijeet Dipke

Born Aurangabad, Maharashtra (1995). Journalism graduate (Pune); MS Public Relations, Boston University. Former AAP digital volunteer (left 2023).

Status

Not ECI-registered

CJP is a citizens’ / youth satirical platform. Leadership has said it has no electoral ambitions and is not “another political party” in the traditional sense.

Identity

Secular · Socialist · Democratic · Lazy

Self-described as a political front of the youth, by the youth, for the youth — reclaiming “lazy & unemployed” as satire with teeth.

Within days the Instagram account crossed millions of followers (reports cite overtaking the BJP’s official IG handle at peak). The X account faced a temporary India block under IT Act provisions and was later restored after Delhi High Court proceedings (July 2026 reporting).
