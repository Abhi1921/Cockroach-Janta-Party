Digital Badge & Civic Rights

Generating your CJP Digital ID Card gives you immediate access to our open-source tools and platforms. Usage and Satire Guidelines

CJP functions as a parody and satirical movement. Your membership card is an artistic credential meant to represent support for student rights and civil transparency. It should not be represented as an official credential of an ECI-registered political party.

We encourage all members to display their badges on their social media handles, use the hashtag #MainBhiCockroach, and actively coordinate local chapters to audit public systems, library hours, and education boards.
