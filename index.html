Core agenda

Five demands.
Measurable outcomes.

Every demand is written so a budget line, a hiring rule, or a public dashboard can prove whether it happened — not whether a speech sounded good.

  1. 01
    Education that publishes its bills

    Fund schools and colleges openly. Publish utilization. Stop treating students like collateral for rankings.

  2. 02
    Jobs with dignity, not unpaid limbo

    Fair wages, fair hiring, and an end to “internships” that are just free labor with a LinkedIn badge.

  3. 03
    Governance you can audit

    If public money moves, the public gets the receipt — contracts, timelines, and owners named.

  4. 04
    Digital rights as citizenship

    Access, privacy, and platforms that serve people — not only ad auctions and Aadhaar panic.

  5. 05
    Youth in the room, not the photo

    If the decision shapes our decade, we belong at the table before the ribbon-cutting.
