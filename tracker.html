A Chronology of Civic Victories

The Cockroach Janta Party Tracker isn't just a list of demands—it's a living record of youth resistance. When the administration attempted to shut down our digital platforms by blocking our social media handles under the IT Act, CJP's legal wing took the matter to the High Court of Delhi. The subsequent ruling, ordering the immediate restoration of our primary handles, represents a historic precedent for satirical political organizations in India.

Similarly, when malicious public interest litigations (PILs) were filed in Uttar Pradesh attempting to ban CJP's street theater and assemblies, our volunteer legal teams successfully defended our constitutional right to assemble, parody, and protest peacefully.

How Tracker Metrics Are Monitored

Unlike government portals that remain stagnant for months, our tracker cell updates items daily. CJP monitors local court registries, press conferences, and parliamentary proceedings. If an administrator fails to deliver on a public promise within the set timeframe, we escalate the item's priority and coordinate offline rallies to increase pressure.

We invite law graduates, researchers, and citizen journalists to join our tracking wing. By monitoring public policy with surgical precision, we turn satirical critiques into concrete legislative and administrative achievements.
